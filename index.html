What to do when you get Covid-19?

Stay home as much as possible, and away from other people. If you live with other people, stay in a specific room and wear a mask around others. Get rest, make sure to stay hydrated. Monitor your symptoms and get help from a healthcare professional if your symptoms get worse. If you have any medical appointments, let your medical professional know you have Covid-19.
